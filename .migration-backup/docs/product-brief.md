# Pulse AI Product Brief

## One-line positioning
Pulse AI is an AI-powered construction intelligence platform that turns fragmented project information into a live operating system for owners, consultants, contractors, approvals, risks, reports, and decisions.

## Target audience
- Government decision makers
- Developers and real-estate owners
- Engineering consultants
- Contractors
- High-net-worth investors
- Construction operators and project directors

## Core story
Construction projects lose time, money, and accountability because critical information is scattered across WhatsApp, emails, PDFs, drawings, BOQs, reports, approvals, and site conversations. Pulse AI centralizes this information, reads it, structures it, detects risks, documents decisions, and gives each stakeholder a live project view.

## Required product modules
1. Landing page / executive story
2. Dashboard / executive health overview
3. Projects list
4. Project detail
5. Project Pulse timeline
6. Tasks and responsibilities
7. Approvals and submissions
8. Reports and AI summaries
9. Floating AI assistant
10. Settings mock page

## Demo data direction
Use realistic UAE/GCC construction data:
- Emirates Coop commercial project
- Residential villas
- Mid-rise residential building
- Authority approvals
- DCD / DM / DEWA style approval stages where relevant
- RFI, drawings, site reports, BOQ, procurement, and delay risks

## Output goal
The result should be credible enough to present as an early product demo to senior owners, government stakeholders, investors, or large developers.
